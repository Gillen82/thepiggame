The Pig Game

Game Rules

  1. Players take turns with a die.
  2. On a player's turn he/she can roll a die as many times as they like.
  3. If a roll is a 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, the player adds that many points to their 'Current Score' for the turn.
  4. A player may choose to end their turn at any time and "bank" their points by clicking the 'Hold' button.
  5. If a player rolls a 1, they lose all their unbanked points and their turn is over.
  6. The first player to reach 100 points or more wins.
